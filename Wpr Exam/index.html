Welcome to Fifi Talks podcast

A Christian-centered series where we dive into raw and unfiltered conversations about faith, culture, and living as a Christian in today’s world. This podcast is all about keeping it real: no sugarcoating, just honest talk about what it truly means to follow Christ in this generation.

In this video, we dive deep into one of the most important and often misunderstood topics in Christian theology: the soul, the spirit, and the flesh. What do these terms mean? How do they interact? And how can understanding them help you walk more faithfully with Christ in daily life? You’ll learn: The biblical definitions of soul, spirit, and flesh The distinctions and overlap between the three How the flesh fights against the spirit (Romans themes, Galatians) How to discern the voice of the spirit vs the pull of flesh Practical steps to cultivate spiritual sensitivity, guard your inner life, and live in victory How this knowledge shapes your identity in Christ and empowers transformation If you’re passionate about knowing who you are inwardly and want to live from a place of spiritual clarity, this video is for you. Don’t forget to like, subscribe, and share your insights: Which aspect (soul, spirit, or flesh) do you struggle with most?
